Data Input guidelines

Tips

  • Sort names are compulsory, but can be identical to the regular name.
  • Regular names are as descriptive as possible, and contain as many references to first and second degree relations as needed to be discriminative.
  • Dates can be partial dates.
  • Productions can have a season assigned to them, if they are (still) conceived in a season-based context. Shows that are linked to the season-specific production must have dates that fall within the season begin and end date.
  • Shows of a production can be in the context of e.g. a festival (which would be a core entity of model Organity, organity_type = Festival
  • ...
